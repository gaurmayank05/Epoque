1. Rap Battle & Beat Box

Short Description

A Rap is a rhythmic written poetry and singing on the stage. Rap should provide a message. through its flow.


Guidlines

  • Time limit: 3-4 Minutes
  • No used Rap is allowed
  • All Rappers have to present their written Rap
  • Participants who are rude, abusive or deemed otherwise unsuitable by the organisers will be disqualified from the competition.
  • All participants must report at the venue at least one hour prior the commencement of the event
  • Number of Participant: 1

2. FOOTLOOSE (Feel the Beats)

Short Description

Tap your feet and unleash your love for dance and enthral the world with your breath-taking moves.


Rules and Regulations

  • Time limit: 4 Minutes
  • Dance performance should not convey any indecent gesture.
  • In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided by the institution, the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the beginning or from the point of disruption that the team wishes, with the consent of the judges.
  • Accessories, props and costumes will be provided by organizers only.
  • All dance forms are allowed.
  • Winners at the Dept. level will perform at the Institute level.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding
  • Violation of any of the above will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Number of Participant: 1.

5. Group Dance (Together we Express and Perform)

Short Description

It is performed by groups of people simultaneously and in coordination with one another.


Rules and Regulations

Time limit: 10 minutes
Choice of songs is open to the participants but dance performance should not convey any indecent gestures
Entries will be selected on the basis of auditions and full performance will be viewed for auditions.
The participants are requested to bring pen drives, CDs etc. to avoid Technical disturbance. Out of which, they are also requested to submit music/ song with the coordinator of the event.
Accessories, props and costumes have to be arranged by participants

8. BAND WAR (Let your Heart Feel the Beats)

Short Description

Band War is a name given to any music contest or competition in which a number of bands compete for the title of "best band".


Rules and Regulations

One entry per college.
Time limit is 08-10 minutes including sound track. Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
Each team may have 04-15 members, including coordinators, Musicians, Lyricist etc.
The participants will have to carry their own instruments. Maximum of 3 instruments are allowed.
Choice/genre of songs is open to the participants. But, any song which has slang or causes any harm to religion, community, cast, creed, society, etc. would be disqualified immediately.
Any kind of harm to the auditorium or stage is strictly not allowed. Lighting a fire, cigarette or using water or alcohol is strictly not allowed in Institution premises.
No props will be provided by the coordinators. Participants can carry their own props.
Participants will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while singing.
The participants will be judged on the basis of their technique, expression, rhythm/ timing, choreography, costumes, makeup, setup and overall effects.
Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
Prizes:
    • Winner: 11,000
    • 1st Runner Up: 7000
    • 2nd Runner Up: Trophy & Certificate

11. NUKKAD NATAK (Come, Act and Inspire)

Short Description

It is a street theatre. It is a form of theatrical performance and presentation in outdoor public spaces without a specific paying audience


Rules and Regulations

A maximum of 15 minutes shall be given including set-up and clearance. Your time starts as you step on the stage, even though for arranging props
Entries will be selected on the basis of auditions and full performance will be viewed for auditions.
Usage of abusive language is strictly prohibited and may lead to disqualification
Performance should be confined within the Nuked area, as defined by the organizers
Teams can use banners and posters. Teams are allowed to use musical props as Dholak, Flute or any other music instrument of their choice
No props will be provided. Teams may use props but need to inform the eventcoordinator beforehand.
It is important for the teams to submit a copy of the synopsis, their name and names of the team members to the organizers.
The main language may be either Hindi or English or a combination of both languages.
Decision of judges will be final and binding.
Violation of any of the above will lead to immediate disqualification.
Number of Participant: Min = 8, Max= 30.

12. SKIT & MIME (Real is rare)

Short Description

A team event meant for a stage play on a perfect platform to showcase a number of skills including acting, scriptwriting and direction.
Mime is a form of silent art that involves acting or communicating using only movements, gestures, and facial expressions. A person performing mime is also called a mime.


Rules and Regulations

Time limit: 15 minutes.
The teams will be free to select the theme/story/script of the skit. However,the script will have to be discussed with the event coordinator before theevent. Theme should not be vulgar & cheap. It should not hurt anycommunity’s feelings.
Entries will be selected on the basis of auditions and full performance will be viewed for auditions.
Participants are required to provide their own costumes, props, sound effects, music accompaniment and make-up
The play may be in English or Hindi.
Decision of the judges will be final and binding
Violation of any of the above will lead to immediate disqualification.
Number of Participant: Min = 8, Max= 20.
